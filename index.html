App Overview

Smart Cast Mirroring is a reliable application designed to stream your smartphone screen to your TV. Whether you are sharing photos, playing games, or presenting a slideshow, our app provides a stable connection for your needs.

  • Performance: Optimized for high-quality visuals with a standard processing latency of approximately 1 second.
  • Compatibility: Fully supports Google Cast, Chromecast, and most modern Smart TVs.

How to Connect via Google Cast

To enjoy your favorite content on the big screen, follow these simple steps:

Crucial Step: Ensure that both your smartphone and your Smart TV (or Chromecast device) are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. The devices will not be able to discover each other if they are on different networks or using mobile data.
  1. Launch the App: Open the Smart Cast Mirroring app on your mobile device.
  2. Scan for Devices: Tap the "Cast" or "Search" icon located on the main screen. The app will scan for available Google Cast-enabled devices.
  3. Select Your TV: Once your TV appears in the list, tap on its name to initiate the connection.
  4. Start Mirroring: Accept any prompts on your TV screen. Your phone screen will appear on the TV shortly.

Troubleshooting

Cannot find your TV in the list?

  • Double-check that Wi-Fi is toggled ON on both devices and they share the exact same network name (SSID).
  • Restart your Wi-Fi router if the devices still cannot see each other.
  • Ensure your TV has Google Cast/Chromecast built-in and it is enabled in the TV's system settings.

Experiencing lag or stuttering?

  • A latency of about 1 second is normal for wireless mirroring.
  • For the most stable connection and to minimize latency, we highly recommend connecting both devices to a 5GHz Wi-Fi band.
